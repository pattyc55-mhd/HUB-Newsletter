HUB NEWS

Coming in April!

The Single Rack Paddling system is coming in April along with new Club Rules signage. The single rack paddling system will:

  • Increase efficiency for waiting paddles

  • Help reduce players queuing up at the incorrect level. 

  • Reduce the possibility of players double-padding.


Rotation on Open Play (non-Challenge Courts) will be 4 on / 4 off, if less than 4 paddles are waiting, all waiting paddles will rotate in. 

Ball Machine Update

For Members bringing in ball machines, if Courts 7 & 8 are not being used, HUB Members can use those courts with their ball machine at no cost. If other players reserve or want to use those courts, the player with the ball machine must get off the court. Courts 15-20 require a paid court reservation for ball machine usage.

TOURNAMENT SERIES REGISTRATION

INFO & REGISTRATION

Come play in these fun and competitive, mixed gender DUPR Round Robin Games. This is a great way to improve the accuracy of your DUPR score or a way to get a new official DUPR rating. Strive to move up to the next level of the ladder and win as many games as possible. Register as an individual. The Ladder is groups of 4 sorted by DUPR Rating.
